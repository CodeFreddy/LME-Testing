3.2.6.3 Credit Risk Add-on46

Credit risk add-on is only applicable to specific CPs who will be notified by HKSCC
individually. Those CPs could refer to the “MTM and Margin Requirement Report” for
the amount to be charged.

For illustration purpose, credit risk add-on for the sample portfolio is assumed to be
12,000,000.

3.2.6.4 Ad-hoc Add-on
Ad-hoc add-on is only applicable to specific CPs who will be notified by HKSCC
individually. Those CPs could refer to the “MTM and Margin Requirement Report” for
the amount to be charged.

For illustration purpose, ad-hoc add-on for the sample portfolio is assumed to be 600,000.
