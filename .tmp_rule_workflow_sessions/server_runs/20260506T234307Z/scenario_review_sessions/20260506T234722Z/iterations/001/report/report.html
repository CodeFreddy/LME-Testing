Title: Calculate flat rate margin with complete three-step process across multiple sub-margin groups
Intent: Verify flat rate margin calculation correctly executes all three steps: position selection by comparing long vs short market values per sub-group, applying flat margin rates, and applying margin multiplier
Priority: high
Case Type: positive
Paragraph IDs: IMHK-3_2_4_2-01
Given
  • A clearing participant has positions identified per §3.2.2 across multiple sub-margin groups
  • Sub-group 1 positions: total absolute long market value is 30,000,000 HKD, total absolute short market value is 60,000,000 HKD
  • Sub-group 2 positions: total absolute long market value is 750,000 HKD, total absolute short market value is 0 HKD
  • Sub-group 3 positions: total absolute long market value is 0 HKD, total absolute short market value is 300,000 HKD
  • Flat margin rates under FieldType 3 are: 12% for sub-group 1, 30% for sub-group 2, 55% for sub-group 3
  • Flat rate margin multiplier of 2 is assigned per Daily Participant Margin Multiplier Report (DWH0081C)
When
  • Step 1: System aggregates and compares total absolute market values of long vs short positions for each sub-margin group
  • Step 1: System includes sub-group 1 short positions (60,000,000 HKD), sub-group 2 long positions (750,000 HKD), and sub-group 3 short positions (300,000 HKD) in calculation
  • Step 2: System sums the product of absolute position market value and flat margin rate for each included position
  • Step 3: System applies the flat rate margin multiplier of 2
Then
  • Sub-group 1: short positions included (60,000,000 HKD), long positions excluded because short absolute value is higher
  • Sub-group 2: long positions included (750,000 HKD), short positions excluded because long absolute value is higher
  • Sub-group 3: short positions included (300,000 HKD), long positions excluded because short absolute value is higher
  • Flat rate margin after applying margin multiplier equals (60,000,000 × 12% + 750,000 × 30% + 300,000 × 55%) × 2 = 15,180,000 HKD
Assumptions
  • Flat margin rates are obtained from FieldType 3 as specified
  • Margin multiplier is obtained from Daily Participant Margin Multiplier Report (DWH0081C)

IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: Similarly for sub-group 2 and sub-group 3 to take the higher of long and short absolute market value for each of them (p.15)
IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: Flat rate margin after applying margin multiplier = (60,000,000 × 12% + 750,000 × 30% + 300,000 × 55%) × 2 = 15,180,000 (p.15)

Scenario ID: TC-SR-IMHK-3_2_8-01-data_validation-01
Title: Validate all risk components are present for total calculation
Intent: Verify that all required risk components are present before calculating total MTM and margin requirement
Priority: high
Case Type: data_validation
Paragraph IDs: IMHK-3_2_8-01
Given
  • A clearing participant has completed calculations under §3.2.5 and §3.2.6
When
  • The system validates input data for total MTM and margin requirement calculation
Then
  • Net margin after credit value is present from §3.2.5.3
  • MTM requirement value is present from §3.2.5.2
  • Position limit add-on value is present
  • Credit risk add-on value is present
  • Ad-hoc add-on value is present
Assumptions
  • Credit risk add-on may not be applicable upon launch of VaR Platform per footnote 46
Evidence
IMHK-3_2_8-01: total MTM and margin requirement could be derived by adding the net margin after credit to other risk components (p.22)
IMHK-3_2_8-01: = Net margin after credit + MTM requirement + Position limit add-on + Credit risk add-on + Ad-hoc add-on (p.22)
IMHK-3_2_8-01: Please note that the credit risk add-on will not be applicable upon the launch of VaR Platform (p.22)
