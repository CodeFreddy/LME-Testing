Given
  • A clearing participant has positions across multiple sub-margin groups defined on HKEX website
  • Sub-group 1: Long positions total absolute market value 30,000,000 HKD, Short positions total absolute market value 60,000,000 HKD
  • Sub-group 2: Long positions total absolute market value 750,000 HKD, Short positions total absolute market value 0 HKD
  • Sub-group 3: Long positions total absolute market value 0 HKD, Short positions total absolute market value 300,000 HKD
  • Flat margin rates under FieldType 3 are 12% for sub-group 1, 30% for sub-group 2, 55% for sub-group 3
  • A flat rate margin multiplier of 2 is assigned per DWH0081C report

  • The flat rate margin calculation is performed following Step 1 (compare long vs short absolute market values per sub-margin group), Step 2 (sum product of included positions' absolute market value and flat margin rate), and Step 3 (apply margin multiplier)

  • Sub-group 1: Short positions included (60,000,000 > 30,000,000), long positions excluded
  • Sub-group 2: Long positions included (750,000 > 0), short positions excluded
  • Sub-group 3: Short positions included (300,000 > 0), long positions excluded
  • Flat rate margin before multiplier = 60,000,000 × 12% + 750,000 × 30% + 300,000 × 55% = 7,590,000 HKD
  • Flat rate margin after applying margin multiplier = 7,590,000 × 2 = 15,180,000 HKD
Assumptions 
Evidence
IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: take the higher of long and short absolute market value for each of them (p.15)
IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: all short sub-group 1 positions will be included in the flat rate margin calculation while all long sub-group 1 positions will be excluded (p.15)
IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: Flat rate margin after applying margin multiplier = (60,000,000 × 12% + 750,000 × 30% + 300,000 × 55%) × 2 = 15,180,000 (p.15)
