  • Sub-group 3 has no long positions and short positions with total absolute market value of 300,000 HKD

  • The system includes only short positions for sub-group 3 (higher absolute market value)
  • The flat rate margin is calculated as (60,000,000 × 12% + 750,000 × 30% + 300,000 × 55%) × 2 = 15,180,000 HKD

  • Margin multiplier is obtained from Daily Participant Margin Multiplier Report (DWH0081C)

IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: Step 2: Sum the product of absolute position market value and the flat margin rate under FieldType 3 (p.15)
IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: Step 3: Apply flat rate margin multiplier by referring to the Daily Participant Margin Multiplier Report (DWH0081C) (p.15)
IMHK-3_2_4_2-01: Flat rate margin after applying margin multiplier = (60,000,000 × 12% + 750,000 × 30% + 300,000 × 55%) × 2 = 15,180,000 (p.15)
