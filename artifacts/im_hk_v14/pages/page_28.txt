Please note that the combination of multi-counter eligible securities positions should be performed after considering SSC / SCC. The resulting positions after the combination of multi-counter eligible securities positions will be used for deriving the marginable position for MTM and margin requirement calculation. 4.5 Corporate Action Position Adjustment When an instrument (a stock) undergoes corporate action(s), it is possible that some benefit entitlements would be distributed to the CPs (shareholders) or would cause a change in number of shares. The market price of the instrument would react to such kind of corporate actions since the ex-date and therefore the original traded positions should be adjusted to capture the changes. It is also possible that more than one corporate action will appear for the same instrument on the different ex-dates.
