Please note that all corporate action position adjustments should be performed after the combination of multi-counter eligible securities positions. There are five types of corporate actions that require position adjustment60: • Bonus share / Stock split / Stock consolidation • Cash dividend • Stock dividend • Rights issue / Open offer • Stock conversion A “Corporate Action Position Adjustment Report” will be generated in csv format and could be downloaded by CPs on each business day61.
